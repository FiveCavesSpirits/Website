The Product

Inspired by Cavehill Faoi Anáil Bheann Mhadagáin

Rising Above Belfast, Cavehill has watched over centuries of history. This gin is a tribute to that enduring presence - infused with botanicals inspired by the mountain's landscape. Unmistakably local, it captures the spirit of the hill and the city it guards - each sip echoing stories of the past, carried through its rugged trails and sweeping views.
